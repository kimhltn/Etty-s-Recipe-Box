# Spanish Basmati Rice — Cook Mode

**Serves:** 4–6 as a side | **Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 20 min | **Rest:** 10 min | **Total:** ~35 min

---

## 🛒 Grocery List

### Produce
- [ ] 1 small yellow onion (need ¼ cup minced)
- [ ] 1 rib celery

### Pantry
- [ ] 1 cup basmati rice
- [ ] ¼ cup tomato sauce (1 small can)
- [ ] 1½ cups low-sodium chicken broth
- [ ] Olive or avocado oil (2 tbsp)
- [ ] Kosher salt

### Spices
- [ ] Onion powder (1½ tsp)
- [ ] Garlic powder (1 tsp)
- [ ] Dried parsley (2 tsp)

---

## Before You Start (2-Minute Prep)

- **Onion:** finely mince ¼ cup.
- **Celery:** finely mince ¼ cup (about 1 rib).
- **Rice:** measure 1 cup basmati into a fine-mesh strainer.

---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Rinse and Toast

1. Rinse **1 cup basmati rice** under cold water until the water runs clear.
2. Heat **2 tbsp olive or avocado oil** in a saucepan over medium. Cook **¼ cup finely minced yellow onion** and **¼ cup finely minced celery** for 2 minutes, then stir in the rice and toast **2–3 minutes**, until lightly golden and fragrant — the toast is where the flavor comes from, don't rush it.

### Step 2 — Season and Simmer

1. Stir in **¼ cup tomato sauce**, **1½ tsp onion powder**, **1 tsp garlic powder**, **2 tsp dried parsley**, and **½ tsp kosher salt**.
2. Add **1½ cups low-sodium chicken broth**, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low **15 minutes**, until the liquid is absorbed.

### Step 3 — Steam and Fluff

1. Off heat, steam with the lid on **10 minutes** — don't peek; the steam finishes the grains.
2. Fluff with a fork and serve.

---

## Tips

- **Born in the caldo:** this is the rice from `recipes/caldo-de-pollo-con-arroz.md` — spoon ¼ cup into each bowl and ladle the soup over.
- **Vegetarian:** swap the chicken broth for vegetable broth, or water plus ½ tsp bouillon.
- **Reheating:** sprinkle a spoonful of water over, cover, and microwave — the steam brings it back.
- **Scaling up:** rice-to-liquid ratio is 1 : 1½; hold the toast time steady and it doubles cleanly in a wider pan.
